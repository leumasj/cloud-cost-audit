3. How We Use Your Email

If you provide your email address, we use it for:

  • Delivering your audit report — sent once, immediately after you provide your email
  • Delivering your Blueprint — sent once after payment is confirmed
  • 90-day re-audit reminders — one email approximately 90 days after your audit, reminding you to re-check for new waste or security issues. Each reminder includes a one-click unsubscribe link

We do not send marketing newsletters. We do not share your email with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not sell email addresses.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

KloudAudit uses localStorage (not cookies) to store a single anonymous session ID in your browser. This ID is a randomly generated string (e.g. ka_1234567890_abc123). It contains no personal information.

We do not use tracking cookies. We do not use advertising cookies. We do not use third-party analytics cookies.

Vercel's infrastructure may set functional cookies for CDN edge caching. These are strictly necessary for the service to function and are exempt from consent requirements under ePrivacy rules.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Right to portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — unsubscribe from re-audit emails at any time using the link in any email we send

To exercise any of these rights, email admin@kloudaudit.eu. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Polish supervisory authority: Urząd Ochrony Danych Osobowych (UODO) — uodo.gov.pl

7. Unsubscribing from Re-audit Emails

Every re-audit reminder email includes a one-click unsubscribe link. Clicking it will immediately remove you from all future reminder emails. No account, no form, no waiting.

You can also unsubscribe by emailing admin@kloudaudit.eu from the address you want removed.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Supabase database access uses row-level security with anon key (read-only for non-admin operations)
  • API endpoints are rate-limited to prevent abuse
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card details
  • Error logs (Sentry) are reviewed regularly and purged

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33.

9. Children

KloudAudit is a professional B2B tool intended for use by adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately at admin@kloudaudit.eu.

10. International Transfers

Some of our processors (Stripe, SendGrid, Anthropic, Vercel, Sentry) are based in the United States. Transfers to these processors are covered by the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, which the European Commission has recognised as providing adequate protection.

11. Changes to This Policy
