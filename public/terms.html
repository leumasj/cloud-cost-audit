2. What KloudAudit Does

KloudAudit provides a self-reported cloud infrastructure audit tool. You answer structured questions about your own cloud setup (provider, bill size, configuration choices). Based on your answers, KloudAudit:

  • Calculates a Waste Score (0–100) and estimated savings range — free, no payment required

Important: KloudAudit never accesses your cloud account, credentials, IAM roles, or billing data. All analysis is based solely on the information you self-report in the audit questionnaire.
